# UI/UX Improvement Priorities

## Implemented (HIGH PRIORITY)

### A. Action-First Pipeline ✅
- Every company card shows exactly ONE clear CTA based on pipeline stage
- Priority-colored action items on Overview page (urgent/medium/low)
- Next-step indicators with deadlines throughout
- Clear "what to do next" on every company detail page

### B. Improved Result Displays ✅
- Human-readable research summaries instead of raw data
- Structured outreach drafts with subject/body/confidence display
- Collapsible sections for technical details and notes
- Confidence bars for outreach draft quality

### C. Inbox Triage Experience ✅
- Replies grouped by urgency: Hot (🔥), Warm (☀️), Cold (❄️)
- One-click actions: Draft Response, Mark Done, Snooze 3d
- Suggested action displayed prominently for each reply
- Collapsible email body to reduce noise
- Status filters (New, In Progress, Snoozed, Done)

### D. Company Timeline View ✅
- Chronological timeline with event-type icons and colors
- Shows: discovery, research, outreach drafts, sends, follow-ups, replies, interviews
- Single source of truth per company
- Metadata display (confidence scores, urgency levels)

### E. Quality Indicators ✅
- Contact confidence badges: Verified (✓ green), Guessed (? yellow), Unknown (! red)
- Outreach draft confidence score with visual bar (0-100%)
- Confidence labels: High/Medium/Low with color coding
- Low-confidence items flagged in company cards

## Future Improvements (MEDIUM PRIORITY)

### F. Drag-and-Drop Pipeline
- Enable drag-and-drop between pipeline stages
- Auto-suggest next actions on stage change

### G. Email Draft Editor
- Inline editing of outreach drafts
- AI-powered suggestions for improvements
- A/B testing for subject lines

### H. Analytics Dashboard
- Response rate tracking by stage
- Time-to-response metrics
- Best outreach patterns

### I. Notification System
- Browser notifications for new replies
- Daily digest email
- Deadline reminders

### J. Keyboard Shortcuts
- Quick navigation between pages
- Action shortcuts (r = reply, d = done, s = snooze)
- Search focus with /
